DevOps culture brings the cohesion of
autonomous teams to fast paced,
high-demanding environments.
It has a set of processes that when
applied correctly can:
• Drastically reduce IT overhead
• Remove manual tasks both of a
mundane or complex nature
• Enforce best security practices
• Expedite product delivery
• Improve communication and
collaboration across teams.

Devops is important to learn for software deployment.DevOps is important because it's a software development and operations approach that enables faster development of new products and easier maintenance of existing deployments.
