George Brown College Casa Loma Campus

George Brown College of Applied Arts and Technology is a public, fully accredited college of applied arts and technology with three full campuses in downtown Toronto. 
Like many other colleges in Ontario, GBC was chartered in 1966 by the government of Ontario and opened the next year. 

At George Brown College, we offer programs that cover a wide range of subject areas and interests designed for diverse learners who come to us from all walks of life.  

You can complete an apprenticeship, earn a diploma or degree, pursue continuing education and more. George Brown has three campuses in Toronto.
